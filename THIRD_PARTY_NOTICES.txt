THIRD-PARTY NOTICES for lathe
=============================

FFmpeg (shared libraries: avformat, avcodec, avutil, swscale, swresample)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
The DLLs distributed alongside lathe.exe (avformat-*.dll, avcodec-*.dll,
avutil-*.dll, swscale-*.dll, swresample-*.dll) are an unmodified LGPL build
of FFmpeg, dynamically linked by lathe.

  License:  GNU Lesser General Public License, version 2.1 or later
            https://www.gnu.org/licenses/old-licenses/lgpl-2.1.html
  Project:  https://ffmpeg.org

Corresponding source code: these binaries are the
"ffmpeg-master-latest-win64-lgpl-shared" build published by the BtbN
FFmpeg-Builds project, which publishes the exact corresponding FFmpeg
source snapshot and build scripts with every release:

  https://github.com/BtbN/FFmpeg-Builds/releases
  https://github.com/BtbN/FFmpeg-Builds

In accordance with the LGPL, the FFmpeg libraries are dynamically linked
and user-replaceable: you may substitute your own compatible builds of
these DLLs next to lathe.exe.

FFmpeg command-line executable (ffmpeg.exe)
-------------------------------------------
Separately from the libraries above, lathe invokes a stand-alone ffmpeg.exe
as an external subprocess for encode/convert operations. That executable is
a GPL build of FFmpeg (https://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl-3.0.html), obtained
and stored independently (see the application's shared tools directory). It
is mere aggregation: it is not linked into lathe, and its source is likewise
available from the projects above.
